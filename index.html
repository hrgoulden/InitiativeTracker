Slow

Target's speed is doubled. It gains a +2 bonus to AC, advantage on DEX saves, and an additional action on each turn. That action can be used to make one weapon attack, dash, disengage, hide, or use an object. After the spell ends, target can't move or take actions until after its next turn.

Target's speed is halved. It takes a -2 bonus to AC and DEX saves, and cannot use reactions. On its turn, it can use either an action or a bonus action, not both, and can't make more than one melee or ranged attack during its turn. If it attempts to cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action, roll a d20. On an 11 or higher, the spell doesn't take effect until the next turn, and the creature must use its action on that turn to complete the spell. Otherwise, the spell is wasted.
